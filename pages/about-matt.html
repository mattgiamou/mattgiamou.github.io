About Matt

I am a student and robotics researcher pursuing a master's of science in aerospace engineering at MIT under Prof. Jonathan How. I received my B.A.Sc. from the University of Toronto's Engineering Science program in 2015, where I majored in aerospace engineering with a robotics and mechatronics minor.

My current research involves the development of an autonomous search and rescue system utilizing multiple UAVs in complex, GNSS-denied environments like beneath a dense forest canopy. Broadly, I am interested in robust robotic systems capable of learning and adapting to their environments.
